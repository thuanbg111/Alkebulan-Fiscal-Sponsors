About Alkebulan Fiscal Sponsors

Alkebulan Fiscal Sponsors is recognized as a 501(c)(3) charitable organization (EIN 86-2489204).

Specific purpose: to engage in charitable activities within the meaning of Section 501 of the Internal Revenue Code, including providing fiscal sponsorship services that enable charitable projects to operate with compliance and accountability.

  • No substantial part of our activities involves lobbying or political campaigning.
  • Assets are irrevocably dedicated to charitable and educational purposes.
  • Upon dissolution, remaining assets go to a similar 501(c)(3).
